The Walimah

The Walimah is the blessed wedding feast celebrated after the marriage contract (nikah). It is a confirmed Sunnah of the Prophet Muhammad ﷺ — an act of gratitude to Allah and a joyful announcement of the union to family and friends. Attending the Walimah when invited is a right of the Muslim upon his brother.

"Give a Walimah feast, even if with one sheep."
— Narrated by Anas ibn Malik (RA): The Prophet ﷺ said to Abd al-Rahman ibn Awf.
Sahih al-Bukhari, Book 67, Hadith 72
"When one of you is invited to a wedding feast, he should attend it."
— Attending the Walimah when invited is a right of the Muslim upon his brother.
Sahih Muslim, Book 16, Hadith 3500
